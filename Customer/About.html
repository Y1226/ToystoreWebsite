4Kids-Plus is an American toy retailer owned by Toys2Games, Inc. (d.b.a. Toys2Games Brands) and various others. The company was founded in 1966; its first store was built in April 1957, with its headquarters located in Parsippany-Troy Hills, New Jersey, in the New York metropolitan area.



4Kids-Plus has a large variety of toys imported from all over, including China, Israel, Japan and the US. The store itself was designed by the best in the interior design field and is designed to give a happy and relaxed atmosphere. There are branches all over the world, allowing all people to have access to the large selection of toys and games, giving everyone the privilege of enjoying the merchandise themselves or rewarding someone with a wonderful gift. For those who do not have a way of getting to the stores there is a clear-cut website, newly renovated and comfortable to use.



4Kids-Plus has millions of happy visitors everyday in every store. Every shopper will come out with a new toy that they are happy with-guaranteed.
